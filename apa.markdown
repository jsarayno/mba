Authors

One or two authors
If a source has one or two authors, you should use all of their names
in both the in-text citation and list of references. For example:
- (McCall & Taylor, 2014, p. 98)
- McCall, J., & Taylor, J. (2014). A guide to computers
(2nd ed.). Palgrave.
